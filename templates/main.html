Unofficial Metlink Wellington Real Time Anywhere

A fast, bookmarkable app for getting RTI info for bus stops in Wellington that don't have displays. Enter the stop number below.

Stop numbers are four-digit numbers that can be found on bus stop signs. For example Courtenay Place Stop A (westbound) is stop number 5000, while stop C (opposite) is stop number 5516.
